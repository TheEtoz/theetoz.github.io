About

What is ETOZ?

A student-led technology community built around one idea: the best way to learn is to build, share, and explore together — with no pressure and no gatekeeping.

🟢

Beginner-Friendly

You don't need experience to join. Ask questions, make mistakes, and learn at your own pace. Everyone starts somewhere.

🔧

Practical

Everything we do connects to real tools and real workflows. No fluff — just hands-on exploration that means something.

🤝

Collaborative

We learn from each other. Students lead sessions, share knowledge, and work on projects together — not in isolation.

📅

Flexible

Join when you can. No attendance requirements or rigid schedules — contribute as much or as little as suits you.

Activities

Two ways to participate

Everything we do falls into one of two modes. Join either, both, or simply observe — there's no wrong way to be part of the community.

// Activity 01

Talk Shows & Mini Workshops

Short, casual sessions where someone shares a concept, tool, or project with the group. Not a lecture — more like an open conversation with a live demo.

  • Anyone proposes a topic — no approval needed
  • Host runs a short, informal walkthrough live
  • Group participates — questions, tries things, discusses
  • Session gets documented and archived on GitHub
// Activity 02

Collaborative Mini Projects

Small groups work together toward a shared technical goal over 1–2 weeks. Projects are intentionally scoped to be completable, not overwhelming.

  • Students self-organise around a shared interest
  • Group agrees on a small, achievable goal
  • Work async via GitHub + short sync check-ins
  • Cycle ends with a group showcase
